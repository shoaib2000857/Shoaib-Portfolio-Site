Shoaib's aesthetic leans toward **scale, mystery, precision, and lore**. His passions (use these to steer visual decisions, never literally): hardcore space/nebula/cosmic person; reading/books; mystery + thriller vibes; hardcore Star Wars fan (the *mythic dark-space, restrained-glow* feel — NOT lightsabers/Vader/franchise art); hardcore cars + fighter/normal aircraft fan (the *cockpit instrumentation, trajectory, engineered-elegance* feel — NOT photos of planes/cars).

He explicitly does NOT want literal/cheesy theming (no planets everywhere, spaceships, book stacks, jets in the hero). He wants the hobbies woven in **subtly** as atmosphere and interaction language.

The chosen identity is **"Celestial Archive / Observatory Noir"** — ~70% technical-cinematic, ~30% dreamy cosmic mystery. See [[shoaib-portfolio-design-system]].
